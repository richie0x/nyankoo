About Nyanko

Nyanko is more than a name; it's a decentralized open-source project inspired by the Japanese folklore kitten.
This digital initiative invites a global community to contribute and explore its enigmatic world. In the spirit of collaborative storytelling, Nyanko provides a platform for enthusiasts and creators to collectively breathe life into its mystique.

Like the enduring tales of Japanese folklore, Nyanko's open-source nature ensures that its narrative evolves and thrives, transcending boundaries and generations, making it a unique and accessible journey for all who wish to partake.
